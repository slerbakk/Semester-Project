Community science museum

Explore together


Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we’re committed to making science accessible to all. Over the course of human history, science has developed from our early understanding of fire, wind, water, and earth to exploring everything from galaxies far away to the very building blocks of life itself.

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us.

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore. Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits – they only come to life at night when everyone’s gone home).





Making science accessible


The Community Science Museum is dedicated to democratizing science education through its multifaceted approach. With a rich array of over 1000 interactive exhibits, the museum transforms scientific concepts into accessible, hands-on experiences, fostering a dynamic learning environment for visitors of all ages. This immersive approach invites exploration into fundamental principles, from Newtonian physics to the wonders of evolution. Specialized programs, including the Young Stars club and school engagements, target younger audiences, instilling a love for learning and discovery.

Ensuring inclusivity, the museum offers free admission, making scientific exploration available to all. Moreover, the incorporation of audio guides, braille displays, and wheelchair accessibility ramps demonstrates a commitment to providing an enriching experience for individuals with diverse needs. By combining engaging exhibits, educational initiatives, and a focus on accessibility, the Community Science Museum is actively shaping a space where science truly becomes a shared adventure, accessible and inspiring for everyone.
